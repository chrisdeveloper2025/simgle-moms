about us

The Single Mother Sanctuary is a dedicated social support group aimed at empowering and uplifting single mothers by providing emotional, practical, and community-based support. This sanctuary exists to create a safe space where women raising children on their own can find relief, encouragement, and resources to thrive personally, emotionally, and economically. Whether a mother is newly single, long-time solo, or facing challenges such as housing instability, financial hardship, emotional stress, or social isolation, the group offers non-judgmental support and a strong sense of belonging. Single mothers face unique challenges that can affect their wellbeing and their children's future.
